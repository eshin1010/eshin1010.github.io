Thomson Reuters - event invitation

Download HTML email

This event invitation email is based off a template used for a while that was previously not responsive. I recoded the template so the right side column stacks underneath for mobile. For the numbered list, I used a table rather than ordered list tag since spacing can be more precisely controlled. Open Sans font falls back to Arial for email clients that don't display web fonts.

The email was deployed with Eloqua. There are personalization tags in the greeting for first name and in the footer for recipient email.

automotiveMastermind B2C

Download HTML email

automotiveMastermind requested emails templates for their client brands, with personalization tags for an individual dealership's name and contact information. The emails are deployed with local dealership information, sent to their customers.

Marketers provided a mockup image, copy, and high resolution banner image.

The side-by-side boxes at the bottom stack for mobile. The left side "Questions" box includes text coded over an image with a fallback dark gray background.

Marketers also requested a plain text version. Though most marketing automation platforms generate plain text versions automatically, the appearance can vary.

This email includes image blocks that are three across for fullsize in browsers and two across for mobile. Montserrat font falls back to Arial where web fonts are not displayed.

The email was designed by an outside agency that provided a PDF mockup. Edits were later made to the bottom section per marketers' design preferences.

The email was deployed in Pardot, with first name personalization tag in the greeting.
