B2C annual transaction review email

View HTML email

Integrating personalization and variables: This extremely detailed email is composed of many elements that combine images and real text, set over background images. It was sent in January to review the past year's transaction history containing eight different personalized content variables for a user's total annual savings, purchases, top brand purchased, and more.

Seamlessly combining real text and images: This features use of background images (in header, $396 savings circle, and bottom two rows of brand circles) and font sizes that vary depending on content (for example, for savings $amount to fit within the yellow circle, a smaller font size is used when the amount is 1,000 or more, and an even smaller font for 10,000 or more, etc.).

Designing and coding for mobile: Designers provided a desktop mockup with placeholder figures that would be replaced with real text depending on user variables. I then reworked the design for mobile to include the graphics, cropped and edited, to be optimized within mobile dimensions.
